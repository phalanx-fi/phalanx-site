Mechanism
How Phalanx works
Phalanx captures the carry — the structural premium that long speculators pay to hold leveraged equity exposure.
01

Deposit USDC

Deposit into any ct- vault. You receive ERC-4626 vault shares representing your proportional ownership. Your exchange rate only goes up as yield accrues.

02

We run the trade

The vault simultaneously buys spot equity on FELIX and shorts the matching perp on HyperLiquid. Delta = zero. Price exposure = zero. Funding flows to you every hour.

03

Withdraw anytime

Burn your ct- tokens to redeem USDC at the current exchange rate. Small redemptions are instant from the vault buffer. Larger ones settle within 30 minutes.

Delta Neutral
Protected by design
Your capital never takes directional risk. Whether NVDA goes to $200 or $50, your vault earns the same carry.

↑ Spot Long

The vault holds the underlying equity token on FELIX. If price rises, the spot position profits.

Long 100 NVDA @ $140 = +$14,000 exposure

↓ Perp Short

Simultaneously, the vault shorts the matching perpetual future on HyperLiquid. If price rises, the short loses — exactly offsetting the spot gain.

Short 100 NVDA-PERP @ $140 = −$14,000 exposure

= Net Delta Zero

The two positions cancel out. The vault has zero price exposure. What remains is the funding rate — paid hourly by leveraged longs to shorts.

Δ = +$14,000 − $14,000 = $0 price risk
